narrado.
Press play on your markdown.
Narrado adds a play button to every markdown editor in VS Code and Cursor.
The document is read aloud with a natural neural voice, generated entirely on your machine.
No cloud, no API keys, no accounts.
Features.
Play button in the editor title bar of any markdown file.
On-device text to speech, free and private.
Live transcript: the line being read is highlighted as it is spoken.
Click any line in the transcript to jump playback there.
Starts speaking after the first sentence, no waiting for the whole document.
Pause, resume, playback speed, and 25 plus voices.
Install.
Download the latest release and install from VSIX.
